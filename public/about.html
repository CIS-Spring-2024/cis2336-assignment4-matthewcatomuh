The SugarLand Cafe

Welcome to The Sugarland Cafe! Here we are the premier food provider of the
Sugarland Campus. I opened this Cafe because when I was a student here I believed
that chips and coffee were not enough food options to feed the nurses, technology,
and Wharton County Junior College students. You must be wondering,
"That's all well and good but, who am I?"

Who Am I?
